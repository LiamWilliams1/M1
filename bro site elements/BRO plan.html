  • Mission Statement

  • The website goal is to increase concert attendance by 35% and to increase awareness about the orchestra and their concerts to potential viewers. The website will also increase the accessability for the audience to provide feedback on what they want.The website will also increase revenue to the organisation by 10%

  • Success Evaluation
  • The website will be a success if the concert attendance increases by 35% or higher.=, and feedbak about the music is increased overall. The website will be a success if the revenue raise increases by 10%.

  • Target Audience
  • The intended target audience is adults aged 45 and over (older people). We wil design the website for the age bracket by making the website classy, prestigous, easily accessible (simple), and spunky. We will do this by making the colours darker and the small amount of text stand out..
